Global supply chains are increasingly vulnerable to adversarial entities — shell companies, sanctioned vendors, and financially distressed suppliers that evade traditional due diligence. Procurement teams at enterprises lack a unified, real-time risk signal that fuses financial health, geopolitical exposure, and entity relationship data.
Build an AI-powered multi-domain data fusion framework capable of detecting adversarial entities in procurement supply chains, with a live-validated, independently reproducible financial health signal module using SEC EDGAR XBRL data.
  • Designed a four-layer ML architecture cross-referencing six data domains: corporate registries, beneficial ownership records, sanctions lists, financial transaction patterns, logistics data, and OSINT feeds

  • Built the module to be independently reproducible and publicly documented
